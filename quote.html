Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive a quote?

We typically respond to quote requests within 24-48 business hours. For more complex projects, we may need additional time to prepare a comprehensive proposal.

What information do I need to provide for an accurate quote?

The more details you can provide about your project, the more accurate our quote will be. Important information includes project scope, desired features, timeline, and budget constraints.

Are there any hidden costs in your quotes?

No, our quotes are transparent and include all costs associated with your project. If any additional costs arise during the project, we'll discuss them with you before proceeding.

Can you work with my budget?

We strive to work within our clients' budgets and can often suggest alternative approaches or phased implementations to accommodate budget constraints while still achieving your goals.

What happens after I accept the quote?

Once you accept the quote, we'll schedule a kickoff meeting to discuss the project in detail, establish milestones, and begin the development process according to the agreed-upon timeline.
