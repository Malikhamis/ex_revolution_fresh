Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services does Ex Revolution Technology offer?

Ex Revolution Technology offers a comprehensive range of services including custom software development, website development, mobile app development, digital marketing (SEO, social media, content marketing, email marketing, PPC), strategic IT consulting, and data analytics solutions. Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their unique needs and deliver tailored solutions that drive business growth.

How long does it take to develop custom software?

The timeline for custom software development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. Simple applications may take 2-3 months, while more complex enterprise solutions can take 6 months or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements. We follow an agile development methodology that allows for regular updates and feedback throughout the process, ensuring the final product meets your expectations.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance services after project completion. We provide different support packages to ensure your software or website continues to function optimally. Our support services include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and technical assistance. We also offer training for your team to ensure they can effectively use the new systems. Our goal is to build long-term relationships with our clients and support their ongoing success.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing is customized based on the specific needs and scope of each project. We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price contracts, time and materials, and retainer arrangements. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs, and we work with clients to find a pricing structure that fits their budget and project requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and quote tailored to your project needs.

Which industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries including retail, healthcare, finance, education, manufacturing, hospitality, and non-profit organizations. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs and challenges of each industry, helping businesses of all types leverage technology for growth. Our diverse experience across different sectors allows us to bring innovative approaches and best practices to every project we undertake.
